About Me

(juh-SAWN) ∇ She/They

Hello! Thanks for dropping in to check out a little more about me.

Ever since I was a tiny human, I have been a creator, first learning to hold a pencil when I was two. I have photos at my grandma's house of me drawing at the table, drawing under the table, and being caught in corners drawing. It's part of who I am, and it's what I LOVE to do. I can usually be found at my desk creating something new or revising something old.

If I'm not working on art, you can find me in the forests, parks, or wherever green things grow. Nature exploration is my other life passion. Standing on the edge of a cliff, feeling the cool wind across my skin, and taking in the beauty of the wondrous pacific northwest I call home.

Currently, my main jam is tattooing at Bitterroot Tattoo in Moscow, Idaho.

My primary mediums are watercolor and ink, but I also enjoy working with acrylic, oil, and digital art. All the mediums have different things that lend themselves better to different tasks.

I have 2 beloved fur babies, Merlin and Kiki. I lost my best friend Jack in January of 2022. You can see him in a couple of different pieces I've made. We miss him every day. Keeping a roof over my kitties' heads, a house full of toys, plenty of catnip, and food in their bellies is what keeps me waking up every day and working.

If you're into witchy things, such as tarot, full moon celebrations, solstice gatherings, making good food, and having small gatherings, we will be fast friends.

I am attempting slow and mindful living. I have stopped living in the fast-paced, rush-to-work, rush-home world. I spend more time in the studio creating, and it is such a privilege to do so. Finding joy in the small and mundane tasks of life has increased my happiness immensely.

I want to contribute more to the community, someday to have a non-profit community arts center, providing valuable resources to young children and folks who are just re-discovering their love of making.

Thanks for reading! Hoping to connect and make art with you and for you soon!
